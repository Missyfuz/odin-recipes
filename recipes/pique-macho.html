Piche Macho

Description

Pique macho is a typical dish from the city of Cochabamba, Bolivia. It was so named because it was said "this pique is for machos" due to the spicy flavor given by the chopped locotos (chili peppers). The traditional recipe of pique a lo macho has minced and seasoned meat, chorizos, potatoes, chopped tomato and locoto. Over the years, this recipe has had variations and has incorporated onion, sausage, chicken, cheese, egg, and other ingredients.
