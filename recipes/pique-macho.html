Piche Macho

Description

Pique macho is a typical dish from the city of Cochabamba, Bolivia. It was so named because it was said "this pique is for machos" due to the spicy flavor given by the chopped locotos (chili peppers).

Ingredients

  • 1 Kilo of pork loin
  • ½ Kilo of sausages
  • 8 medium potatoes
  • 4 large onions
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 8 Locotos (relatively spicy fruits) replace with the chili pepper of your choice
  • Oil
  • Ground pepper
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground garlic
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into small pieces and season with salt, garlic, pepper and cumin to taste.
  2. Chop the locoto into small cubes and sauté in a pan with a little hot oil, with the onion chopped into feathers, the diced tomato and a pinch of salt.
  3. Separately, fry the meat, covering it to obtain juicy meat.
  4. Brown the potatoes in oil, cut them into long pieces.
  5. Mix everything together and add the sliced sausages and french fries.
  6. Serve garnished with pieces of raw locoto chopped in long strips.

Hope you enjoyed!
