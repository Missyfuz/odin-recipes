Chicken Milanesa

Description

Chicken milanesa, a delicious dish, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • one egg
  • A spoonful of parsley
  • Flour, as needed
  • Bread crumbs, as needed
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Oil, as needed

Steps

  1. Filleting the chicken breasts
  2. Salt and pepper to taste
  3. In a bowl, beat one egg
  4. Add parsley
  5. Flour the chicken fillets
  6. Then dip these fillets in the egg with parsley and dredge them in the breadcrumbs.
  7. Fry them in a pan with oil until they are cooked.
  8. Serve with rice, lettuce and sliced tomatoes.
